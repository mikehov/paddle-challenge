Why start surfing?

Besides that surfing is a very fun activity to do outdoors, it’s also good for your physical and mental health. It releases stress and tensions and can be very calming. It’s a different way off enjoying the nature and taking a step back from what we call “reality”, we are basically connecting with our nature. While surfing you will discover a new parts off life you have probably not experienced before, discovering new muscles you didn’t know you had, finding out new skills and techniques, maybe even making some new friends along the way. Surfing is quite challenging, but we will get better and better at it along the way. Still haven’t convinced you yet? That’s oki, it’s maybe not everyone's cup of tea, but surfing is a lot of fun and has a lot of benefits.

What are the benefits?

Physical

Surfing has many physical benefits if done correctly. It’s a physicall sport where you will use and train muscles at places you didn’t know you had them in the first place. Core muscles like: your arms, shoulders, chest, back and legs will be heavily targeted. Besides the strength that you gain while surfing and paddling, your also improving your balance, stamina, flexibility and lung capacity to. You can safely say, surfing is a real work out!

Mental

Not only physical benefits, yes also mental benefits. Even though surfing is considered to be a real exercise, it's also very calming and feels very relieving. No reason to think about work, no reason to think about the struggles you are dealing with, just you and nature enjoying having a great time. Not only surfing it's self, but just watching the waves hitting the coast can be really relaxing. Just go with flow!

Confidence

As most people know, surfing ain't easy. Surfing can also to be considered, quite painful and even a little dangerous. You will learn to sometimes face your fears, believe in yourself and go for it. As Roy T. Bennett once said: "The one who falls and gets up is stronger than the one who never tried, do not fear failure but rather fear not trying." The longer you surf, the more confidence you will build, that's a beautiful skill we need life that will makes us, the better version of ourselves.

Nature

Sometimes we forget how beautiful our mother earth actually is, you will look at our nature at ways you have maybe never looked at before. The beautiful waves, the lovely birds in the sky, it makes us think twice about respecting our earth. Nature will make us feel connected where we can be our selves, doesn't matter who we are or where we come from.

People

Surfing is something you can enjoy with our without friends. But most of the time, you will face some other surfers. Doesn't matter if know you them, you will both enjoy the waves and maybe have little talk and maybe even share a little drink in the end. Those are people just like you with different lives, believes, norms and values that enjoy hitting a wave from time to time.
